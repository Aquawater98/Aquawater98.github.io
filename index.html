Hi! My name's Eileen, and I'm currently a backend software engineer at Square in San Francisco. I work on the Customers Directory team, which allows merchants to track and manage their customers. I received my B.S. in Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University in May '17.

One of my recent projects involves developing an algorithm to automatically identify and merge together duplicate customer profiles in Directory. This involves finding linkages via email and phone between customer profiles and merging the ones with a high degree of confidence into a common profile. Merchants are able to better identify who their buyers are and consolidate all the information for their customers, which is especially helpful for merchants whose customer count ranges in the tens or hundreds of thousands.

Another one of my recent projects was creating a profile attachments feature in Directory. This feature allows merchants to attach PDFs or images to customer profiles. For example, a hair salonist could attach an image of a client's hair style to reference during the next appointment, or a driving school could attach permission slips as PDFs on customer profiles. This aligns with Directory's mission to allow merchants to convey all necessary information attached to a customer online instead of on paper.
